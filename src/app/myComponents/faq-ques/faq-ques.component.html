Payments

Your order will be placed and processed, irrespective of the online payment confirmation. In case of any unsuccessful payment/s, you can retry the payment or pay online later. Subsequently, you can also tap on the “Pay Now” button on the homepage or go to the order details page to make a payment. If you are facing a payment confirmation problem with your order, please note that online payment confirmations are completely dependent on the issuing bank. However, if the payable amount has been deducted from your account but shows as transaction failed, the amount will reflect in your account/wallet within 7-10 business days.

Once the refund has been initiated, the refund amount is expected to reflect as per the payment method:
Cash on Delivery - Refund will be in Bank account (Within 24 hours)
Online Payment via Credit / Debit Cards - Refund will be credited to the source (3 - 5 business days)
Bank Transfer - Refund will be in the updated Bank account (7 - 10 business days)
Net banking - Refund will be in Net Banking account (7 - 10 business days)
UPI - Refund will be in Net Banking account (5 - 7 business days)
PhonePe & other E-wallets - Refund will be in PhonePe & E-wallets (1 - 2 business days)
NEFT (Bank Refund) - Refund will be credited to your Bank Account (5 - 7 business days)
